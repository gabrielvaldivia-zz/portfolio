From Rafa to mobile to connected cars, I'm energized by change & emerging technologies.

I'm a Cuban Product Designer based in New York, currently exploring how technology could make people safer at Jigsaw. Prior to that, I explored social VR and content creation at Facebook, and connected cars at Automatic.

I'm passionate about design mentorship, empowering diverse perspectives, and creating transformative experiences through the power of storytelling.
