Gabriel Valdivia
Essays Work Experiments

Gabe is a mission-driven design leader with experience building teams & products for startups and large organizations alike.

In the past, Gabe has led the design of products in a multitude of industries, most notably in social good, visual storytelling, emerging technologies, and smart hardware. From Virtual Reality to Mobile to connected cars, Gabe is energized by how design and technology can enact positive change.

Currently based in New York City, Gabe was born in Cuba and grew up in Costa Rica before moving to Florida to complete his BFA in Graphic Design. He uses his background to bring a unique perspective to products and teams by seeking and empowering diversity, public speaking, and design mentorship.
